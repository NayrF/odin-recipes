"Cinnamon Toast"

Soft, Fluffy, and Delicious!

For this simple and quick "Cinnamon Toast" recipe, you'll need just a handful of kitchen essentials!

  • Prefered Bread
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Eggs
  • Blueberries

Lets Get Cooking:

  1. Begin by soaking your bread in a mixing bowl with an egg for each piece of toast being made.
  2. Place your stove on medium heat, and set your toast evenly spaced on a non-stick cooking pan.
  3. Evenly cook toast on both sides until bread begins to rise and turn light brown.
  4. At this point, you're ready to pour on 1/2 cup of ground cinnamon.
  5. Pour 1 tablespoon of Vanilla on each piece of toast.
  6. Using your hands only, grab 2 hand fulls of Blueberries, and squeeze them together over your toast.
  7. At this point, serve your handcrafted CINNAMON TOAST to the lucky, hungry soul.
