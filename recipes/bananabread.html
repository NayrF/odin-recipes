Banana Bread

Perfect rise, to this delicious bread!

This easy to make and deliciously sweet banana bread, is sure to be a novel for any bakers recipe book!

But before we give away the routine, go ahead and pick up these simple ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Bananas
  • Buttermilk
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Chopped Nuts (if desired).
  1. Begin by moving oven rack to a low position so that tops of pans will be in center of oven. Heat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease bottoms only of 1 loaf pan, 9x5x3 inches.
  2. Mix sugar and butter in a large bowl, then stir in eggs until well blended. Add bananas, buttermilk and vanilla, then beat until smooth. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt until moistened. Stir in nuts, then pour into pans.
  3. Bake 8-inch loaves for about 1 hour, 9-inch loaf about 1 1/4 hours, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Let it cool for 10 minutes. Loosen sides of loaves from pans; remove from pans and place top side up on wire rack. Cool completely, about 2 hours, before slicing. Wrap tightly and store at room temperature up to 4 days, or refrigerate up to 10 days.
